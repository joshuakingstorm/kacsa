About KACSA

Kawanda Christian Students Association (KACSA) is the Scripture Union fellowship at Kawanda Secondary School. We are part of the wider Scripture Union movement in Uganda, which focuses on evangelism, discipleship, and mission among children and youth.

Our fellowship brings together Christian students from all denominations to worship, pray, study God's Word, and support one another in our spiritual journey. We believe in the transforming power of the Gospel and seek to be agents of positive change in our school and community.

Our Purpose

KACSA exists to:

  • Help students develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ
  • Provide a supportive Christian community within the school
  • Equip students to live out their faith in daily life
  • Reach out to fellow students with the love of Christ
  • Serve the school and local community

Our Activities

We organize various activities throughout the school term including:

  • Weekly fellowship meetings with worship and Bible teaching
  • Prayer meetings and Bible study groups
  • Evangelistic outreach events
  • Community service projects
  • Leadership training programs
  • Annual retreats and conferences

Join Us

All students are welcome to join our fellowship regardless of denominational background. Whether you're a new believer or have been walking with Christ for years, there's a place for you in KACSA.

Our meetings are held every Wednesday at 4:00 PM in the School Chapel. Come and experience the joy of Christian fellowship!
